## Getting Started

### What is KEPROBA Trade Directory?
The Kenya Export Promotion and Branding Agency (KEPROBA) Trade Directory is the official platform connecting international buyers with verified Kenyan exporters. Find quality products, connect with reliable suppliers, and grow your business with Kenyan exports.

### Who Can Use This Platform?
- International buyers and importers
- Procurement managers
- Business development professionals
- Trade organizations
- Anyone seeking Kenyan products

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Stable internet connection
- Valid email address

## Registration & Account Setup

### Step 1: Access Registration Page
1. Visit the KEPROBA Trade Directory website
2. Click **"Register"** button in top navigation
3. Or click **"For Buyers"** on homepage
4. Select **"Buyer"** as account type

**Screenshot Location:** Homepage with registration options

### Step 2: Complete Registration Form

#### Personal Information
**Required Fields:**
- **First Name:** Your first name
- **Last Name:** Your last name
- **Email Address:** Your business email (used for login)
- **Phone Number:** Include country code (e.g., +1 for USA, +44 for UK)
- **Password:** Minimum 8 characters
  - Must include uppercase letter
  - Must include lowercase letter
  - Must include number
  - Must include special character

**Screenshot Location:** Registration form

#### Company Information (Optional but Recommended)
- **Company Name:** Your organization name
- **Country:** Your country of operation
- **Industry:** Your business sector
- **Website:** Company website URL

**Why Provide Company Info?**
- Builds trust with exporters
- Faster response to inquiries
- Better search results
- Professional profile

**Screenshot Location:** Company information section

### Step 3: Email Verification
1. Check your email inbox
2. Look for email from noreply@keproba.go.ke
3. Click verification link
4. Redirected to login page
5. Account is now active

**Didn't Receive Email?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 5 minutes and check again
- Click "Resend Verification Email"
- Contact support if still not received

**Screenshot Location:** Email verification message

### Step 4: First Login
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click **"Login"** button
4. Welcome to your dashboard!

**Screenshot Location:** Login page

## Searching for Exporters

### Accessing the Directory

**From Homepage:**
1. Click **"E-Directory"** in navigation
2. Or click **"Browse Directory"** button

**From Dashboard:**
1. Click **"Directory"** in sidebar
2. Browse all verified exporters

**Screenshot Location:** Directory page layout

### Search Interface Overview

**Main Components:**
- **Search Bar:** Top of page
- **Filters Panel:** Left sidebar
- **Results Grid:** Center area
- **Map View:** Toggle option
- **Sort Options:** Dropdown menu

**Screenshot Location:** Full directory interface

### Using the Search Bar

**Basic Search:**
1. Type product name, company name, or keyword
2. Press Enter or click search icon
3. Results update automatically

**Search Examples:**
- "coffee beans"
- "tea exporters"
- "fresh flowers"
- "avocado"
- "handicrafts"

**Search Tips:**
- Use specific product names
- Try different keywords
- Use singular and plural forms
- Check spelling

**Screenshot Location:** Search bar with example

### Filtering Results

#### Location Filter
**Filter by County:**
1. Click **"Location"** accordion
2. Scroll through Kenyan counties
3. Check boxes for desired counties
4. Results update automatically

**Popular Locations:**
- Nairobi (business hub)
- Mombasa (port city)
- Nakuru (agriculture)
- Kisumu (lake region)

**Screenshot Location:** Location filter expanded

#### Export Markets Filter
**Find exporters already serving your region:**
1. Click **"Export Markets"** accordion
2. Select your country/region
3. See exporters with experience in your market

**Benefits:**
- Proven export experience
- Familiar with regulations
- Established logistics
- Market knowledge

**Screenshot Location:** Export markets filter

#### Industry/Sector Filter
**Filter by Business Type:**
1. Click **"Industry/Sector"** accordion
2. Select relevant sectors:
   - Agriculture
   - Horticulture
   - Tea & Coffee
   - Manufacturing
   - Textiles
   - Technology
   - Handicrafts
   - And more...

**Screenshot Location:** Industry filter
