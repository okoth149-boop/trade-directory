## Getting Started

### What is KEPROBA Trade Directory?
The Kenya Export Promotion and Branding Agency (KEPROBA) Trade Directory is an official platform connecting Kenyan exporters with international buyers. As an exporter, you can showcase your business, products, and certifications to reach global markets.

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Stable internet connection
- Valid email address
- Business registration documents (PDF format)

---

## Registration & Account Setup

### Step 1: Access the Registration Page
1. Visit the KEPROBA Trade Directory website
2. Click on **"Register"** or **"For Exporters"** button
3. Select **"Exporter"** as your account type

**Screenshot Location:** Homepage with Register button highlighted

### Step 2: Fill Registration Form
Complete the following fields:

#### Personal Information
- **First Name:** Your legal first name
- **Last Name:** Your legal last name
- **Email Address:** Business email (will be used for login)
- **Phone Number:** Include country code (+254 for Kenya)
- **Password:** Minimum 8 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, and numbers

#### Business Information
- **Business Name:** Official registered business name
- **Business Type:** Select from dropdown (Limited Company, Partnership, Sole Proprietor, etc.)
- **KRA PIN:** Your business KRA PIN number
- **Year Established:** Year your business was founded

**Screenshot Location:** Registration form with all fields visible

### Step 3: Verify Your Email
1. Check your email inbox for verification link
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be redirected to the login page
4. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check spam folder

**Screenshot Location:** Email verification message

### Step 4: First Login
1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **"Login"**
3. You'll be directed to your dashboard

**Screenshot Location:** Login page

## Business Profile Management

### Accessing Your Business Profile
1. From the dashboard, click **"Business Profile"** in the sidebar
2. Or click **"Complete Your Profile"** banner if shown

**Screenshot Location:** Dashboard with Business Profile menu highlighted

### Profile Sections

#### Section 1: Basic Details
**Required Fields:**
- **National ID:** Your Kenyan National ID number
- **Business Name:** Auto-filled from registration
- **Company Logo:** Upload PNG/JPG (max 5MB)
  - Click "Upload Logo" button
  - Select image from your computer
  - Logo will be displayed on your public profile

**Tips:**
- Use a high-quality logo with transparent background
- Recommended size: 500x500 pixels
- Ensure logo is clear and professional

**Screenshot Location:** Basic Details section with logo upload

#### Section 2: Business Details
**Required Fields:**
- **Type of Business:** Limited Company, Partnership, etc.
- **Year Established:** Year business was founded
- **Number of Employees:** Select range from dropdown
- **KRA PIN:** Business tax identification number
- **Sector/Industry:** Select your primary industry
- **Business Organization:** Select relevant organization membership

**Optional Fields:**
- **Sub-sector:** More specific industry classification
- **Annual Revenue:** Select revenue range

**Screenshot Location:** Business Details section

#### Section 3: Documents
**Required Documents (PDF only, max 10MB each):**

1. **Registration Certificate**
   - Click "Upload Registration Certificate"
   - Select your business registration PDF
   - Wait for upload confirmation
   - Green checkmark indicates successful upload

2. **PIN Certificate**
   - Click "Upload PIN Certificate"
   - Select your KRA PIN certificate PDF
   - Wait for upload confirmation

**Optional Documents:**
3. **Export License**
   - Upload if you have an export license
   - Not required but recommended

**Important Notes:**
- All documents must be in PDF format
- Maximum file size: 10MB per document
- Documents are reviewed by KEPROBA admin for verification
- Keep original documents for reference

**Screenshot Location:** Documents section with upload buttons

#### Section 4: Location & Contact
**Required Fields:**
- **License Number:** Your export license number (if applicable)
- **Town/City:** Your business location
- **County:** Select from dropdown
- **Physical Address:** Complete street address
- **Contact Phone:** Business phone number
- **Company Email:** Business email address

**GPS Coordinates (Required):**
- Click **"Get Current Location"** button to auto-fill
- Or manually enter latitude and longitude
- Format: -1.2921, 36.8219 (Nairobi example)
- Coordinates help buyers find you on the map

**Screenshot Location:** Location section with GPS button

#### Section 5: Social Media & Website
**Optional Fields:**
- **Website URL:** Your business website
- **Facebook:** Facebook page URL
- **Twitter/X:** Twitter profile URL
- **LinkedIn:** LinkedIn company page URL
- **Instagram:** Instagram business profile URL

**Tips:**
- Include full URLs (https://www.example.com)
- Ensure social media pages are active and professional
- These links appear on your public profile

**Screenshot Location:** Social Media section

#### Section 6: Export Information
**Required Fields:**
- **Current Export Markets:** Select countries you export to
  - Click dropdown to select multiple countries
  - Use search to find countries quickly
  - Selected countries appear as tags

**Optional Fields:**
- **Target Export Markets:** Countries you want to export to
- **Export Experience:** Years of export experience
- **Certifications:** Add your business certifications

**Screenshot Location:** Export Information section

#### Section 7: Company Story
**Optional but Recommended:**
- Write a compelling description of your business (max 1000 characters)
- Include:
  - What makes your business unique
  - Your mission and values
  - Quality standards
  - Why buyers should choose you

**Tips:**
- Be professional and concise
- Highlight your competitive advantages
- Mention any awards or recognitions
- Use proper grammar and spelling

**Screenshot Location:** Company Story text editor

### Adding Certifications

1. Scroll to **"Certifications"** section
2. Click **"Add Certification"** button
3. Fill in certification details:
   - **Certification Name:** e.g., "ISO 9001:2015"
   - **Issuing Organization:** e.g., "Kenya Bureau of Standards"
   - **Issue Date:** When certification was issued
   - **Expiry Date:** When certification expires
   - **Certificate Number:** Unique certificate ID
   - **Certificate URL:** Link to verify certificate (optional)
   - **Upload Certificate:** PDF or image of certificate

4. Click **"Add"** to save
5. Repeat for additional certifications

**Screenshot Location:** Add Certification dialog

### Saving Your Profile

**Important:**
- Click **"Save Changes"** button at the bottom of each section
- Wait for success confirmation message
- Green toast notification confirms save
- You can save sections individually or all at once

**Navigation Between Sections:**
- Use **"Previous"** and **"Next"** buttons
- Or click section tabs at the top
- Red asterisk (*) indicates required fields
- You cannot proceed to next section until required fields are filled

**Screenshot Location:** Save button and navigation buttons

## Managing Inquiries

### What are Inquiries?
Inquiries are formal business requests from buyers interested in your products or services. They appear in your Inquiries section and Messages.

### Viewing Inquiries

1. Click **"Inquiries"** in sidebar
2. See list of all inquiries with:
   - Buyer name and company
   - Inquiry date
   - Status (New, Read, Replied)
   - Unread message count
   - Preview of message

**Screenshot Location:** Inquiries list page

### Responding to Inquiries

#### Method 1: From Inquiries Page
1. Click on inquiry card
2. Opens chat interface
3. Type your response in message box
4. Click **"Send"** or press Enter
5. Message is sent to buyer

**Screenshot Location:** Inquiry detail with chat

#### Method 2: From Messages
1. Click **"Messages"** in sidebar
2. Select conversation
3. Reply in chat interface

### Inquiry Best Practices

**Response Time:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Quick responses improve your reputation
- Buyers appreciate prompt communication

**Professional Communication:**
- Use proper grammar and spelling
- Be courteous and professional
- Provide detailed information
- Include pricing when requested
- Mention minimum order quantities
- Clarify payment and shipping terms

**What to Include:**
- Product availability
- Pricing (FOB, CIF, etc.)
- Minimum order quantity
- Lead time for production
- Payment terms
- Shipping options
- Certifications and quality standards
- Samples availability

**Screenshot Location:** Sample professional response

### Attaching Files to Messages

1. Click **"Attach"** button (paperclip icon)
2. Select file from computer
3. Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC, XLS
4. Max file size: 10MB
5. File uploads and appears in chat
6. Buyer can download the file

**Use Cases:**
- Product catalogs
- Price lists
- Certificates
- Technical specifications
- Sample images

**Screenshot Location:** File attachment in chat

## Verification Process

### Why Verification Matters
- Verified badge increases buyer trust
- Higher visibility in search results
- Access to premium features
- Official KEPROBA endorsement

### Verification Requirements

**Required Documents:**
1. ✓ Business Registration Certificate
2. ✓ KRA PIN Certificate
3. ✓ Complete business profile
4. ✓ At least one product listed
5. ✓ Valid contact information

**Optional but Helpful:**
- Export license
- Business certifications
- Quality standards documentation

### Verification Steps

#### Step 1: Complete Your Profile
1. Fill all required fields in business profile
2. Upload all required documents
3. Add at least one product
4. Save all changes

**Screenshot Location:** Profile completion checklist

#### Step 2: Submit for Verification
1. Go to **"Business Profile"** page
2. Click **"Submit for Verification"** button
3. Confirm submission
4. Status changes to "Pending Verification"

**Screenshot Location:** Submit verification button

#### Step 3: Admin Review
- KEPROBA admin reviews your profile
- Checks document authenticity
- Verifies business information
- Review takes 3-5 business days

**You'll receive email notifications about:**
- Verification in progress
- Additional documents needed
- Verification approved
- Verification rejected (with reasons)

#### Step 4: Verification Outcome

**If Approved:**
- ✓ Verified badge appears on profile
- ✓ Email confirmation sent
- ✓ Higher search ranking
- ✓ Access to premium features

**If Rejected:**
- Email explains reasons
- List of issues to fix
- Resubmit after corrections

**Screenshot Location:** Verified badge on profile

### Maintaining Verification

**Annual Renewal:**
- Verification valid for 1 year
- Renewal reminder sent 30 days before expiry
- Update documents if needed
- Resubmit for renewal

**Verification Can Be Revoked If:**
- Documents expire
- Business information is false
- Complaints from buyers
- Violation of terms of service

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Cannot Login

**Problem:** "Invalid email or password" error

**Solutions:**
1. Check email spelling (no spaces)
2. Verify password (case-sensitive)
3. Click **"Forgot Password"** to reset
4. Check if email is verified
5. Clear browser cache and cookies

**Screenshot Location:** Login error message

#### Document Upload Fails

**Problem:** "Upload failed" or "File too large"

**Solutions:**
1. Check file format (must be PDF)
2. Verify file size (max 10MB)
3. Ensure stable internet connection
4. Try different browser
5. Compress PDF if too large
6. Rename file (remove special characters)

**Screenshot Location:** Upload error message

#### Profile Changes Not Saving

**Problem:** Changes disappear after refresh

**Solutions:**
1. Click **"Save Changes"** button
2. Wait for success confirmation
3. Check internet connection
4. Fill all required fields (marked with *)
5. Try saving one section at a time

**Screenshot Location:** Save confirmation message

#### Not Receiving Inquiry Notifications

**Problem:** Missing email notifications

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add noreply@keproba.go.ke to contacts
3. Verify email in profile settings
4. Check notification settings
5. Ensure email address is correct

**Screenshot Location:** Notification settings

#### Verification Taking Too Long

**Problem:** Pending verification for over 5 days

**Solutions:**
1. Check email for admin requests
2. Verify all documents uploaded correctly
3. Ensure profile is 100% complete
4. Contact admin via support email
5. Check verification status in profile

**Screenshot Location:** Verification status

#### Cannot Add Products

**Problem:** "Add Product" button not working

**Solutions:**
1. Complete business profile first
2. Upload required documents
3. Verify email address
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try different browser

#### Messages Not Sending

**Problem:** Message stuck on "Sending..."

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh page
3. Try sending again
4. Check file attachment size (max 10MB)
5. Clear browser cache

**Screenshot Location:** Message sending status

## Appendix

### Glossary of Terms

**HS Code:** Harmonized System code for international trade classification

**FOB:** Free On Board - seller delivers goods on board vessel

**CIF:** Cost, Insurance, and Freight - seller pays for shipping and insurance

**MOQ:** Minimum Order Quantity - smallest order accepted

**Lead Time:** Time from order to delivery

**KRA PIN:** Kenya Revenue Authority Personal Identification Number

**2FA:** Two-Factor Authentication - extra security layer

**Verification Badge:** Official KEPROBA endorsement symbol

Before submitting for verification, ensure you have:

- [ ] Business Registration Certificate (PDF)
